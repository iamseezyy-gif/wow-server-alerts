History of World of Warcraft
---
---

Classic Era (Vanilla)
- Azeroth recovers from the Orc Wars
- The Horde and Alliance in a fragile peace
- Threats: Ragnaros, Onyxia, Nefarian

The Burning Crusade
- The Dark Portal reopens
- Burning Legion invades
- Blood Elves and Draenei unite

Wrath of the Lich King
- Arthas awakens as the Lich King
